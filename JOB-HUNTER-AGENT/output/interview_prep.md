# Interview Prep: ASML – AI Engineer

## Job Overview
ASML is recruiting a Mid-level AI Engineer to join its Computational Imaging & AI team in Eindhoven (remote‐friendly). You will research, develop, and deploy deep learning models for real-time image reconstruction and process control in EUV/DUV lithography scanners. The role demands proficiency in Python, TensorFlow/PyTorch, C++/CUDA, GPU acceleration, and experience optimizing ML algorithms on embedded systems. You’ll collaborate with optics, firmware, mechatronics, and system‐integration teams to push the boundaries of semiconductor manufacturing.

- **Tech Stack Match**: Expert in Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, C++, CUDA/OpenCL—mirrors required tech.
- **Deep Learning & Image Reconstruction**: Built CNNs and thermal-imaging models, optimized inference pipelines on GPU-embedded platforms.
- **Embedded & Production Focus**: Designed PyVISA/KSSD TSP control flows and GPU-accelerated modules for real-time R&D instruments.
- **Cross-Functional Collaboration**: Proven track record working with hardware engineers, R&D scientists, and global teams.

## Strategic Advice
- **Tone & Focus:** Be confident, technically precise, and collaborative. Highlight curiosity about both algorithmic innovation and production constraints.  
- **Storytelling:** Use STAR (Situation, Task, Action, Result) to frame achievements—focus on measurable impact (e.g., 80% time savings, 10% accuracy gain).  
- **Alignment:** Weave in ASML’s values—demonstrate a growth mindset (Challenge), show examples of cross-discipline teamwork (Collaborate), and reference sustainability or quality focus (Care).  
- **Red Flags to Avoid:**  
  • Vague answers on end-to-end deployment—be specific about your role in CI/CD, testing, and integration.  
  • Overemphasis on prototype vs. production—ASML values durable, high-reliability solutions.  
  • Underplaying semiconductor domain knowledge—review basic lithography terms and workflows.  

Good luck! You’re well-equipped to showcase both your deep technical expertise and your fit with ASML’s mission and team culture.
