**2026 Personal Health & Fitness Goals**

**Vision:**
Build a leaner, stronger, and healthier body through consistent training, disciplined nutrition, and long-term sustainable habits.

---

# 1. Body Composition Goal

Primary objective for 2026 is to improve overall body composition.

Goals:

* Reduce **7 kg of body fat**
* Maintain or increase **lean muscle mass**
* Achieve a stronger and more athletic physique

Strategy:

* Maintain consistent resistance training
* Prioritize adequate protein intake
* Track body weight and physical condition regularly

Progress Tracking:

* Weekly body weight monitoring
* Visual and strength progress tracking
* Monthly reflection on adherence and progress

---

# 2. Strength Training & Muscle Growth

Focus on **hypertrophy (muscle growth)** and progressive strength development.

Training Structure:

* **Weight training at least 6 times per week**
* Target: balanced full-body development
* Emphasis on progressive overload

Key Focus Areas:

* Back and pull strength
* Upper body hypertrophy
* Overall muscular development

Strength Milestone Goal:

* Perform **3 strict pull-ups**

Training Philosophy:

* Consistency over perfection
* Small improvements accumulated over time
* Avoid injury by managing recovery and fatigue

Current Status (March 4, 2026):

* Average training frequency: **~7 sessions per week**
* Overall adherence to training plan: **~80%**

---

# 3. Nutrition & Recovery

Daily Nutrition Target:

* **Protein intake: 110–120 g per day**
* Emphasis on whole and nutrient-dense foods
* Maintain balanced calorie intake to support fat loss while preserving muscle

Habits to Maintain:

* Regular meal structure
* Avoid excessive processed foods
* Maintain adequate hydration

Recovery Focus:

* Prioritize sleep quality
* Manage training fatigue
* Support recovery through nutrition and supplementation

---

# 4. Supplement Strategy

Creatine Supplementation:

* Started **Creatine Monohydrate in the last week of February 2026**
* Purpose:

  * Improve strength performance
  * Support muscle growth
  * Enhance training capacity

Strategy:

* Daily consistent intake
* Combine with resistance training and adequate hydration

Expected Benefits:

* Slight increase in training performance
* Improved muscle recovery
* Potential increase in lean muscle mass

---

# 5. Lifestyle Discipline

Build sustainable habits that support long-term health.

Key Principles:

* Discipline over motivation
* Consistency over intensity
* Focus on long-term progress rather than short-term results

Maintain:

* Regular training schedule
* Balanced nutrition
* Structured lifestyle habits

---

Overall adherence to goals: **~80%**

Positive Indicators:

* Training frequency exceeding goal (**~7 sessions per week**)

Main Focus Moving Forward:
